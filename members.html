Site membership form
Do you have the facilities and ability to deliver General Paediatric research at your site.  If you would like your department to be one of our member sites you can apply using this form.
Study proposal form
Do you have an idea for a General Paediatric research study that needs a multi-centre approach?  Bring it GAPRUKI’s attention using this form.  There is usually a call for proposals prior to GAPRUKI meetings, at which potential studies are discussed in detail.  However, we are happy to receive proposals at any time, and if their subject matter is felt urgent enough to warrant it, they may be circulated outside of meeting times.
